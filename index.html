About Attorney Fernando Gutierrez

Attorney Fernando Gutierrez is a Chicago native and the son of Mexican immigrants. With over 10 years of experience in immigration law, he understands firsthand the challenges families face when navigating the complex U.S. immigration system.

Built on a foundation of honesty and integrity, the Law Offices of Fernando Gutierrez provides expert legal representation to families across Cook County and the Chicago metropolitan area, helping them protect their rights and stay united.
